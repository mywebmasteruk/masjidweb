FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Setup and Launch

What’s included in the Full Setup Service?

Our Professional Setup service includes everything needed to get your site launched and looking great. We input all of your details and content, we’ll add your logo, and customize colors. We’ll add your photos, media files connect social media accounts, etc

How long will it take to create my website?

Ususally, we can have your new website ready in 10 business days.

I have my own domain name. Can I use that?

Yes, when you register, we’ll give you a temporary domain, like yourname.masjidweb.com. Then once we have your new website all setup and ready to go live, we’ll connect your own domain.

I don’t have a domain name yet, how can I order one?

For additional cost of £20 per year. We can register a domain name for you. Please let us know when you sign-up.

Please note - domain names are subject to availabilty. So while we will do our best to find you the ideal domain name, it really depends on whether the domain name you chose is still available.

Can I use my current web host?

masjidweb.com provides a hosted service, with web hosting included. So you won’t need to pay for your own hosting after you launch your website with us.

My masjid has multiple locations. Can we list them all on our website?

Yes, you can, at no extra charge. However, if you’re doing Online Booking system, then it costs a bit more per location. Contact us for further discussion.

What modules are included?

All plans come with following modules included: Prayer Times, News/Blog, Events, Location Map, Donations, Social Media Integration, Courses, Email Subscription, Ad space, Booking form, Service page, About-us page, Photo Gallery, Video/Audio Gallery, Job listing page. Anything else? just ask...

The monthly service fee covers your web hosting, security maintenance, and access to all current and new features built into your site. It also includes our efficient technical support, which is what our customers really love about our service.

We’re here to help you succeed. You’ll have the ability to log in and make content updates to your site anytime you want. But if you get stuck, our experts are just an email away.


Updating your website

Can I make updates myself?

Yes. You will have the ability to login and make edits to your site anytime you want. For example, changing menu items, photos, info – all very easy to do. We’ll train you and offer PDF tutorials and videos where available. And of course, we’re here to help answer questions anytime.

Can you make updates for me?

Yes, if you need our help, just send us an email to support@masjidweb.com and we’ll do the update for you (no extra charge). We can make most routine updates for you within 1-2 business days.

General Contract Terms

Are there any contracts?

When you sign up with us, you automatically go on our month-by-month core plan. You can cancel at anytime. However, the longer you commit the more you can potentially save. For long-term stability, we recommend the 48 month plan, it can save you up to 20% on the monthly support fees. Please visit our pricing page for more details.

What happens if I decide to leave?

We would of course be sad to see you go. However if you make the difficult decision of leaving us, you need to give us 30 days notice. and you can start the process by contacting our support team

Can I take my website files when I leave?

Unlike other providers, we don’t lock you in, once your plan term ends, we offer the option for a departing client to take a copy of their website with them.

How much notice do I need to give for Departure?

You must give us at least 30 days notice for us to process your Departure.

If I choose to leave, is there any cacellation fees to pay?

In most cases, No,there are no fees or penalties for cancelling your service. However, if you request a copy of the website we built for you, and you have been a customer with us for less than 12 months, we would normally charge a Departure Fee. Please contact us for more details.
